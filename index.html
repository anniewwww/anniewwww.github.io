Mechanical Optimization

Overview: Optimization of an aerospace mounting bracket where weight reduction was critical for fuel efficiency.

Methodology: Using SolidWorks Simulation, I performed topology optimization to remove material from low-stress zones.

Results: The part was 24% lighter than the baseline with a 1.62 safety factor, validated through physical testing.
